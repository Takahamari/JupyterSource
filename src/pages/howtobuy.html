How Trade Jupyter on Pancakeswap

Step 1.Open the link

Click I Understand

then click Import

Step 2.Connect Wallet

TThere are various options to connect to Wallet, for example, if you have a Web3 extension then you can directly connect to it, or if you have installed Trust wallet on your phone you can click directly on Trust wallet. In this tutorial, I`m showing how to connect from PC to Trust Wallet on the Phone

Click Connect Wallet

Click More

and click Wallet Connect

Scan the QR Code from the Trust Wallet app on your phone.

Step 3. Buy $IOM

After successfully connecting your wallet,now you need to input how much BNB are you going to swap, and then click Swap

Click Confirm Swap

Then head back to the phone and Confirm the transaction, by clicking on Confirm.

And that`s it, you have successfully bought $IOM, the next currency on Jupiter.
