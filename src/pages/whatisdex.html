What is a DEX

DEX stands for Decentralized EXchange

A decentralized application is a smart contract-based software application that stores data on the blockchain.

Because DApps do not force you to expose your identity, you will be able to maintain a certain amount of privacy.

The graphical user interface (GUI) of decentralized applications is optional, although any developer can construct his own GUI and communicate with the application`s smart contract.
